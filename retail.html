Digital skin printing

"Digital skin printing" is a term that doesn't have a widely recognized definition in common industry contexts. However, it could refer to the use of digital printing technology to create patterns, images, or designs on a material that is meant to resemble skin or a flexible, thin surface. Digital printing is a method of printing from a digital-based image directly onto various media. It typically involves processing the artwork or design through a computer and then using a printer to transfer the design onto a surface .

Shop Fascia

A shop fascia is a sign or panel that runs along the front of a shop, typically positioned above the entrance or windows. It generally displays the name of the shop, along with any branding elements such as a logo or taglines. Fascia signs are designed to attract attention and communicate the identity of a business, often playing a key role in a shop's curb appeal and street presence.
